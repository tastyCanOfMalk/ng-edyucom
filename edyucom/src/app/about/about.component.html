What I'm about.

I'm a chemist turned coder!


As a chemist, I worked with a lot of data. Actually.. I collected messy numbers and copy-pasted them into 80's-era Excel spreadsheets. It was terrible and tedious, but also inspirational.

I picked up scripting languages to automate data collection and calculations. I learned to automate plot creation and reports. After automating the boring stuff, I created dashboards to track and make predictions about the business. I was really into it.

Fast forward to today and I'm holding a Masters in Data Analytics and currently working as a Full Stack Developer! You'll find a few projects here using skills I've picked up along the way.

Thanks for looking!
