What I'm about.

I'm a chemist turned coder!

As a chemist, I enjoyed the automation of our, admittedly, outdated data methods more than the actual chemistry itself. When you have to process heaps of raw data and present it in a meaningful way, it pays to be lazy and streamline the process!

The presentation of said analyses were also something I took a shining to, ultimately steering me to pursue a Masters of Data Analytics at Georgia Tech. You'll find some projects here using skills I've picked up along the way.
